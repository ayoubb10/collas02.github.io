What is our Project About ?

Our project contains active listening because it involves listening to different people in your school community and trying to understand their perspectives and experiences. It also relates to school because it helps you learn more about the topics that are relevant to your education, such as challenges, achievements, goals, or interests. By practicing active listening, you can improve your communication skills, academic performance, and personal growth.

  • Learn More

Project objective !

- Explore the benefits and challenges of active listening in schools.

- Propose effective strategies and activities for teaching .

- Paying attention to a speaker and listening to understand, not to respond .

- Foster positive relationships and collaboration in the classroom .

- Investigate how active listening can be taught and learned in schools .

Who are our Targets !

Active listening is more than just hearing words; it is understanding the meaning and intent behind them. My project examines how active listening can be used in diverse scenarios, such as personal, professional, and academic settings. The objective is to show how active listening can help achieve various outcomes, such as building rapport, resolving conflicts, and acquiring knowledge to different targets :

Students

Active listening is a valuable skill for students, as it can help them improve their academic performance, social interactions, and emotional well-being.

  • Details

Teachers

Teachers can benefit from active listening skills, as they can enhance their classroom management, instruction, and assessment. By practicing active listening, teachers can foster a positive learning environment, deliver more engaging lessons, and provide more effective feedback to students.

  • Details

Business-man

Business-men need active listening skills to succeed in their careers, as they can improve their negotiation, persuasion, and leadership abilities. By practicing active listening, business-men can build trust, rapport, and influence with their clients, partners, and employees.

  • Details

Footballers

Footballers can improve their performance and teamwork by practicing active listening skills. Active listening can help them understand the instructions, feedback, and strategies of their coaches and teammates. It can also help them communicate more effectively with referees, opponents, and fans.

Project Schedule !

Frequency: 2 times a week

Duration: one month
